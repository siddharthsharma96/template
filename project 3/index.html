Our Mission

At Severo Health we're on a mission to transform how telehealth is delivered and administered by healthcare providers globally.
At Sevaro Health we offer unique products that are designed by telehealth professionals to enable health care providers to offer telemedicine services to their patients. We have created unique solutions and accelerators with out product set for Urgent Cares, Neurology and Primary Care. We will soon be adding new solutions for other specialities. Products
Emergency Car
Our Emergency Care products are used by EMS and ER staff for Video Encounters with on call specialists and offer end to end monitoring from the time EMS responders reach the the patient. The platform offers metric tracking, real time alerts and consult note automation. We currently offer a robust Telestroke solution and are adding Cardiology, Pediatrics, Radiology, GI, Dentistry, Orthopedics and many other specialties.
Outpatient Clinics and Inpatient Rounding
Our products enable complete out patient clinics with remote doctors. Doctors are able to independently conduct clinics and create consult notes and orders. Coupled with our carts, our products are used for bed side patient in rounding and bed side specialist consults in collaboration with the hospital staff.
In Home Consultation
Our platform enables patients to do follow up consults from the comfort of their home. This increases patient retention and reduces re-admissions. Additionally, our product allows the patients to secuerly upload their health records, chose specialists and initiate new consults: all directly from their home

Our Solutions
Urgent Care Solutions
Our Urgent Care Solution helps you increase your revenue by offering Speciality consults to your patients. The solution can also be used for improving efficiency, allowing your physicians to do remote consults. This allows you to operate your urgent care for longer hours with a smaller support staff. Follow up consults can also be done by the patient from the comfort of their home.
Group Practice Solutions
Our group practice solutions are especially effective for multi location practices. They allow you to operate in a hub and spoke model giving your patients a choice of location to see any physician any day. It allows for location agnostic specialist consults within the group and also increases revenue by enabling new network referrals. It also helps optimize the support staff utilization.
Speciality Solutions
We offer speciality solutions for Neurology, Neurosurgery and Neuropsychology. Our solutions include speciality specific features for patient intake and consult notes e.g. TPA Eligibility and auto calculation of NIHSS scores. We are soon introducing speciality solutions for Cardiology, Pediatrics, Radiology, GI, Dentistry, Orthopedics and many other specialties.
Sevaro Virtual Consult Room
Our solution allows for setting up of virtual consult rooms for primary care or specialist consults. This rooms can be located in Urgent Cares, Group Practices, Corporate Wellness Centers and Large Events. Our consult rooms can utilize your existing network and hardware or optionally can be set up completely independently with our video consult carts. This greatly reduces the wait times for the patients, increases physician utilizations and provides medical coverage in hard to reach areas.
Sevaro Multi Office Solution
With our multi office solutions, group practices can operate in a hub and spoke model. It allows the practices to have more in practice specialist referrals and consults. It removes the physical presence barriers where physicians can provide coverage to specific locations on specific days. It allows the practices to have more specialist offerings and the patient to have a one-stop experience for all their needs.
